Who We Are

A movement led by students, for the underprivileged

Born during the COVID-19 crisis, NayePankh has grown into one of India's largest student-led organizations — turning compassion into consistent, on-ground action.

🍲

Food Distribution

Regular meal drives for underprivileged families and even stray animals, ensuring no one goes hungry.

🩷

Health & Hygiene

Awareness campaigns and free sanitary napkin distribution to support menstrual health among women and girls.

📚

Education Support

Helping underprivileged children access basic education and learning resources for a better future.
